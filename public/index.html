Show your focus by growing a garden!

Focus Garden is a minimalist web app that turns your focus time into something you can see and grow. Each time you start a focus session, a seed begins to sprout. Stay on task, and it blossoms; get distracted, and growth pauses. Your garden becomes a visual record of your consistency and discipline.
