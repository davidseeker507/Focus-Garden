Show your focus by growing a garden!

Focus Garden is a minimalist web app that turns your focus time into something you can see and grow. Each time you start a focus session, a seed begins to sprout. Stay on task, and it blossoms; get distracted, and growth pauses. Your garden becomes a visual record of your consistency and discipline.

Designed to keep you present.

Focus Garden weaves together mindful productivity and gentle game mechanics.

Plant-powered Pomodoros

Choose focused sessions that match your energy. Short sprints grow sprouts, deep work nurtures towering trees.

Mindful interruptions

When you leave the session screen your plant pauses growth. Return quickly and your momentum continues.

Visual habit tracker

See patterns in your focus over days and seasons. Celebrate streaks, reclaim calm when you need a break.
